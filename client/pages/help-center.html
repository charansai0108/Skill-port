Help Center

Find answers to common questions and get the support you need to make the most of SkillPort.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Get Started" button and follow the registration process. You can choose between a personal account or community account.

What's the difference between personal and community accounts?

Personal accounts are for individual learners, while community accounts are for organizations, universities, or groups that want to manage multiple users.

How do I join a community?

You can join communities through invitations from community admins or by searching for public communities in the communities section.

Learning & Practice

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically tracked as you solve problems and complete challenges. View detailed analytics in your dashboard.

Can I practice offline?

Currently, SkillPort requires an internet connection. We're working on offline capabilities for future releases.

How do contests work?

Contests are timed competitions where you solve problems against other users. Check the contests section for upcoming events.

Account & Billing

How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to your account settings and select "Upgrade Plan" to choose a Pro or Enterprise subscription.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No long-term contracts required.

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password via email.
