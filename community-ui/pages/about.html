About SkillPort

SkillPort is a comprehensive platform designed to revolutionize how developers and learners track their progress, participate in coding communities, and showcase their skills to the world.

Our Mission

To provide a unified platform where developers can track their learning journey, participate in meaningful contests, build professional communities, and create impressive portfolios that showcase their expertise.

What We Offer

  • Personal Task Tracking: Kanban-style task management for organized learning
  • Coding Stats Integration: Real-time stats from LeetCode, GitHub, GFG, and more
  • Community Management: Create and manage coding communities
  • Contest Platform: Participate in coding challenges and competitions
  • Public Profiles: Showcase your skills and achievements
  • Auto-Validation: Automatic assignment validation through browser extensions

Our Story

Founded by developers who experienced the challenges of tracking progress across multiple platforms, SkillPort was born from the need for a unified solution. We believe that every developer deserves a platform that not only tracks their progress but also connects them with like-minded individuals and opportunities for growth.
